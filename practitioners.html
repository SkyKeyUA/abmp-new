Professional Liability Insurance You Can Trust

In an accident you call us first, not an insurance company-we don't leave you to face a difficult situation alone. With ABMP you have individual liability insurance coverage, so all policy limits are yours and not subject to a master policy limit. There's no extra charge for Additional Insured Endorsements, and you can even add optional Business Personal Property insurance to protect your equipment.

Unlimited FREE CE, No Strings Attached

Want to keep your skills sharp and learn new techniques? The ABMP Education Center gives members 400+ free CE hours from on-demand courses from top instructors like Ruth Werner, Whitney Lowe, Erik Dalton, and Allison Denney. Plus, ABMP Certified members get even more, with an additional 375+ free CE hours.

Expert Assistance at Your Fingertips

Our members-only, quick-reference apps are fun to use and full of information you can use in your practice.
